About Dr. Sarah

Medicine Practised
with Purpose

Dr. Sarah Khan is a Lahore-based consultant physician in Internal Medicine with more than 15 years of clinical experience. She completed her MBBS from King Edward Medical University and FCPS in Internal Medicine from the College of Physicians and Surgeons Pakistan.

Her philosophy is simple: excellent medical care begins with deep listening, thorough evaluation, and shared decision-making. She integrates the latest evidence-based guidelines with genuine empathy to provide treatment that respects the whole person.
